About University

Misr University for Science & Technology (MUST) is the first private university established in Egypt with nearly 20,000+ students. Since 1996, MUST has been recognized for effectiveness and notability of its academic programs as well as the success of its sustainability efforts. Must is well known for its dedication to excellence. MUST is an active member of the Association of Arab universities, the International Association of university Presidents and the Association of African Universities.
