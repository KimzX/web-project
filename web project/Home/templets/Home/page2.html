College of Applied Health Science Technology (AHST)

About College

The Faculty of Applied Health Sciences Technology at Misr University for Science and Technology seeks to provide students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical values. This goal is achieved by attracting elite faculty members while utilizing advanced educational programs to prepare the students for their professional careers, whether in Radiology and medical imaging technology, respiratory care technology, medical laboratory technology and Health Administration and Informatics Technology, The ultimate goal is to satisfy the local and regional labor market needs in the health sectors besides providing scientific research that serves society and keeping pace with the present and the future requirements in compliance with quality standards.

The Faculty of Applied Health Sciences Technology is dedicated to a variety of academic programs that meet the needs of society, preparing qualified technical professionals in the fields of Radiology and Imaging Technology, Medical Laboratory Technology, Respiratory Care Technology, and Health Administration and Informatics Technology ( HAIT).
